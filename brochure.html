About
CrimeLens

CrimeLens is an AI-powered surveillance platform that analyzes live and recorded camera feeds using Computer Vision and deep-learning models to automatically detect suspicious and criminal activity in real time — scoring each event by priority and raising instant alerts to security teams through connected web and mobile dashboards.

The problem it solves

Manual monitoring of camera feeds is slow, tiring and error-prone — causing delayed response to incidents. CrimeLens replaces continuous human watching with automated, real-time detection while keeping a human in control of every critical decision.

What it does

Key Features

  • Weapon & violence detectionFlags guns, knives and physical assault as they happen.
  • Suspicious behavior recognitionDetects loitering, intrusion and abnormal activity.
  • Priority-based scoringEvery event is ranked so urgent cases surface first.
  • Instant smart alertsReal-time push to web and mobile the moment it triggers.
  • Live multi-camera streamingMonitor many feeds at once from one place.
  • Role-based accessOfficer & dispatcher roles with secure permissions.
The workflow

How It Works

  1. 1
    CaptureCameras stream live video feeds into the platform.
  2. 2
    AnalyzeCV & deep-learning models scan each frame for threats.
  3. 3
    ScoreDetected events are ranked by severity and priority.
  4. 4
    AlertInstant notifications reach the right team members.
  5. 5
    RespondSecurity acts on incidents from the live dashboard.
Always in the loop

A human stays in control of every critical decision — AI assists, people decide.

Under the hood

AI Engine & Tech

At its core, CrimeLens runs a real-time computer-vision detection engine served through a fast Python API, with a Laravel + React control plane and a Flutter companion app — all containerized and cloud-scalable.

  • Deep-learning detection models
  • Containerized & cloud-scalable
  • Reliable incident data store
